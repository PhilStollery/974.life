SAFER TOGETHER

We love fireworks and we know that lots of our town do as well. We also know that without our usual display, people will launch their own. However, fireworks can be disturbing for pets, wildlife, farm animals and people. Getting everyone to launch at an appropriate time together reduces any nuisance caused.

Before using fireworks make sure you have read the firework code on the ROSPA website. Stay safe!

For those with pets the RSPCA have some tips here on how to look after pets during fireworks.
