7pm Thursday 5th November

If you and your family are going to be enjoying some fireworks, we're asking you to consider launching them at 7pm on the 5th of November.

ALL TOGETHER

Tune into edge.radio from 6.30pm and wait for the countdown to launch.

Edge radio will be playing a stonking backing soundtrack to all the explosions and we'll bring you details about how each year we make the fireworks happen.

SAFER TOGETHER

By synchronising Wotton's fireworks to happen at the same time we as a town will reduce the harm caused to pets. When lighting your fireworks always stay safe and follow the firework code.

For pet owners the RSPCA have some guidance on how to reduce the harm to your pets here.

RAISE TOGETHER

Together we make Wotton great, you help us raise thousands for our local community ever year.

As this year we can't hold out normal event, if you'd like to give us a donation you can here.

PLAY TOGETHER

If you're male and aged between 18-45 you can join us on every 2nd and 4th Tuesday in the month. Sometimes we got to the pub, sometimes rock climbing, sometime we organise amazing events like the Wotton Drive-in Cinema.

About Wotton-under-Edge and District Round Table 974

Round Table is part of a family of organisations that try to bring people together socially, and at the same time, to give back something to the community.
